Overview

Tushar Spring Works, established in 2008, is a leading manufacturer of precision-engineered springs and wire forms based in Nashik, Maharashtra. With over a decade of dedicated service and a state-of-the-art 5000 sq. ft. production facility, we cater to diverse industrial needs with reliability and precision.

From automotive and electricals to agriculture machinery and general engineering, our springs are trusted components across India. Backed by modern machinery, skilled workforce, and a commitment to quality, we are known for fast turnarounds, custom solutions, and robust spring performance.

We currently offer more than 4000 spring variants, and our ability to deliver import-substitute solutions has made us a valuable partner for manufacturers across sectors.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision
To become India’s most trusted and innovative spring manufacturer, delivering world-class quality and engineering excellence in every product.

Our Mission
To deliver customized, reliable spring solutions that meet exacting standards To continually upgrade technology, processes, and skills To maintain a customer-centric approach, ensuring satisfaction through timely delivery and quality performance To contribute to the Make in India initiative by providing efficient import alternatives
