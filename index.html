Welcome to
Tushar SPRINGS

ISO 9001:2015 Certified

Tushar Spring Works, established in 2008, is a leading manufacturer of precision-engineered springs and wire forms based in Nashik, Maharashtra. With over a decade of dedicated service and a state-of-the-art 5000 sq. ft. production facility, we cater to diverse industrial needs with reliability and precision.

From automotive and electricals to agriculture machinery and general engineering, our springs are trusted components across India. Backed by modern machinery, skilled workforce, and a commitment to quality, we are known for fast turnarounds, custom solutions, and robust spring performance.

We currently offer more than 4000 spring variants, and our ability to deliver import-substitute solutions has made us a valuable partner for manufacturers across sectors.

Industry We Served

Auto & Auto Ancillaries

The auto ancillary industry manufactures and supplies parts, equipment, and chemicals used in automotive systems and assemblies.

Power & Energy

Springs are used in watches, radio-powered systems, door locks, and other energy-conversion mechanisms.

Electrical & Home Appliances

Used in a variety of devices such as electronics, medical devices, toys, and home appliances for control, actuation, and resistance.

Petroleum & Refinery

High-resilience springs for seals, valves, and couplings
Corrosion-resistant components for harsh chemical environments

Material Handling & Engineering

Load-bearing springs, wire forms for jigs and fixtures
Safety components for industrial machinery.

Hardware Furniture and Lock

From toys and wind-up mechanisms to door locks and furniture, springs serve critical motion and control roles.

Textiles

Used in tension control and suspension systems in textile machinery and road vehicles via air springs and coils.

Luggage

Durable and flexible springs are used in modern luggage for handles, locks, and structural support mechanisms.

Consumer Products

Compression springs are used across mechanical and consumer goods such as pens, toys, mattresses, and switches.
